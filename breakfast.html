French Toast 🍞

Ingredients Method
  • 2 Slices of Bread
  • 1 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 tsp Milk
  • 2 Large Eggs
  • 2 tbsp Cream
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Essence
  • 2 tsp Cinnamon Sugar
  • Honey
  1. Crack 2 large eggs into a bowl and combine it with your vanilla essence and milk
  2. Add oil to the pan and allow the oil to heat up
  3. While the oil is heating up dip your slices of bread into your egg mixtire
  4. When the oil is hot, add your slices of bread into the pan. Be sure to drain excess egg mixture. Flip to cook on both sides.
  5. Once the bread is cooked move to a plate and sprinkle 1 tsp of cinnamon sugar on each slice of bread
  6. Drizzle honey all over the french toast, and add 1 tbsp on top of each slice of bread and enjoy

Curried Scrambled Eggs 🥚

Ingredients Method
  • 2 Large Eggs
  • 1 tsp Milk
  • 2 tbsp Oil (Preferably Vegetable)
  • 1 Clove
  • 1 Bay Leaf
  • 1 Elachi Pod
  • 1 Small Stick of Cinnamon - NB: Not Cinnamon Powder
  • 1⁄2 Small Onion (Chopped Finely)
  • 11⁄2 tsp Chilli Powder
  • 1⁄4 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala
  • 1 tsp Roasted Masala (Optional)
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  1. Set the pan to a medium heat, and then add your oil and allow the oil to heat up
  2. Add your finely chopped onions, as well as the clove, elachi, bay leaf and stick of cinnamon. Allow that to braise for about 5 min. If the onions are charring too quickly, add a dash of water to slow the cooking process and to prevent burning. While the onions are braising beat the eggs with some salt, pepper and your milk
  3. After the onions have braised, add your spices and mix well. You can add 2 tbsp if it is too dry
  4. Once the spices has been mixed with all the onions, add your butter. And once the butter has melted add your eggs and start scrambling.
  5. Serve the eggs with some bread, roti, wraps etc. and enjoy

French Toast Bites 🍞 🍯

Ingredients Method
  • 4 Slices of Bread
  • 1 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 tsp Milk
  • 2 Large Eggs
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Essence
  • 2 tsp Cinnamon Sugar
  • Honey
  1. Cut off the crusts if your bread, and then cut your bread into 6 squares
  2. Crack 2 large eggs into a bowl and combine it with your vanilla essence and milk
  3. Add oil to the pan and allow the oil to heat up
  4. While the oil is heating up dip your slices of bread into your egg mixtire
  5. When the oil is hot, add your slices of bread into the pan. Be sure to drain excess egg mixture. Flip to cook on both sides.
  6. Once the bread is cooked move to a plate and sprinkle cinnamon sugar all over the bites of french toast
  7. Drizzle honey all over the french toast and enjoy

Muesli and Yoghurt

Ingredients Method
  • Fruit of Your Choice
  • Muesli
  • Yoghurt
  • Honey
  • 1 tbsp Pumpkin Seeds
  1. Make a layer of fruit at the bottom of your dish. Make sure to cut fruits such as:
    • Apples
    • Peaches
    • Bananas
    • Pears etc.
    into thin slices
  2. Layer your Full Fat Yoghurt
  3. Throw about a 1⁄4 cup of Muesli on top of the yoghurt
  4. Spread your pumpkin seeds, drizzle with honey and enjoy
